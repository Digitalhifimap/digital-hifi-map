About The Map

zThis is a digital map of Historic Filipinotown, as you can see. Historic Filipinotown is a neighborhood situated just west of Downtown Los Angeles, and it's also known as HiFi.

However, in recent years, the area has grown very much subject to gentrification, which is civic redevelopment that can—and in this case, very much does—displace existing residents, removal of culture, and removal of place.

This digital map aims to preserve and make accessible the culture and history of HiFi, and seeks to answer questions like "How do we continue learning about HiFi and begin building further awareness of displacement and gentrification?" and "How do we make these realities more visible and accessible?"

The goal of this map is to serve as a platform for users of all backgrounds to begin exploring and appreciating Historic Filipinotown, acting as a gateway for further learning on how displacement and gentrification affect the neighborhood.

This map was created in collaboration between the 2026 Community Media Lab interns of Search to Involve Pilipino Americans and members of the HiFi community, particularly Gerald Gubatan, a highly knowledgable HiFi local, advocate, and city planner. Thank you deeply, Gerald!
